FAQs

Power outlets are 120 volts which are the same as in the United States.

Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between 12:00 a.m. and 9:00 a.m.

The drinking age is 18 years old.

Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English.

Very little English is spoken in the rural areas.

There is one hospital and many clinics.

The hospital has many multilingual employees.

Violent crime is very rare.

Taniti enjoys a large number of national holidays, and many tourist attractions and restaurants will be clsoed on holidays so visitors should plan accordingly.

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar as it currency, but many businesses will alsoaccept euros and yen.

Several banks facilitate currency exchange, and many businesses accept major credit cards.
